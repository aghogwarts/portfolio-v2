An early 5am start, cold air cutting through the ghats. The kind of ride where you stop halfway not because you're tired, but because you have to — the view demands it. Reached the fort at sunrise, the valley still wrapped in mist below. Stayed up there for an hour doing nothing in particular. Some rides are about the destination. This one was about the 6am silence at 1200 metres.
